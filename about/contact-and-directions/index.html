DIRECTIONS

Getting here by train:
Blackburn Train station is only a pleasant 7 minute walk through the Cathedral Quarter and has regular services from across the North West

Getting here by bus:
Blackburn Bus Station is a 5 minute walk and any bus headed for Blackburn Town Centre should be OK

Getting here by bicycle:
There are secure short and long stay cycle lockers at Waves Water Fun Centre and Blackburn Railway Station free of charge and monitored by CCTV.
We can accommodate 1 or 2 bicycles in the Pop-up for short periods of time.

Getting here by car:
The closest car park is at The Blackburn Mall which has entrances at both Astley Gate – BB2 2DQ (coming from South or West) and Richmond Terrace/Exchange Street – BB1 7JN (coming from North or East).

There is also a multi-storey car park at Fielden Street – BB2 1LQ about a 4 minute walk.

Wheelchair Access:
Our door on Museum Street is accessible by wheelchair. Please ring us on 01254460150 when you arrive and we will come and put the ramp down.
