Community Makers

Home / Community Makers

What is it?

Community Makers is a collaboration between Chip In and The Making Rooms. We aim to assist and mentor volunteers in the borough to learn practical technical skills.
With these skills, they can create useful projects that support community organizations.

Who can join?

If you are aged 16-30, live within Blackburn with Darwen and have an interest in volunteering locally

Where is it?

The workshops will be taking place at The Making Rooms. The Making Rooms is a creative technology hub where we can teach you how to laser cut, 3D Print and more. You can make everything from led signs to mascots and merhandise.
