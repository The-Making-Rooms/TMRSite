Summer Studio – 2D Manufacture

Harness the powers of the laser cutter and see your designs leap from your screen as real objects.

Join us for an introduction to making in the Making Rooms.

Ages 16–24  |  lunch provided  |  12 noon – 4pm  |  lunch provided
Whole programme: £5  |  Individual workshops: £2
