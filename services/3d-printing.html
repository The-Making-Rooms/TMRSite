3D Printing


Whether you need a functioning prototype, a replacement part, a jig or fixture, or an innovative sales & marketing gizmo we have a range of 3D Printing machines and materials to suit.

Our local, fast and reliable service means we can usually turn around your order in a matter of days and are always available to discuss designs, material suitability and even help with correcting faulty 3D models before they go to print.

With over 20 different materials available, sizes ranging from tiny detailed items to large industrial components (up to 406 x 406 x 355 mm), and over 20 years of combined experience we can usually find the right material and process for your 3D print.
